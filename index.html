prepocalypse

If your world ended tomorrow, would you be ready?

Preppers call it TEOTWAWKI: the end of the world as we know it. It feels like an apocalypse and actually it is so.
Many phenomena lead us to reason about the reality of an apocalypse: earthquakes, fires, social disorders, nuclear attacks or even cyber.
Being a prepper means anticipating the events considering their risks, needs and solution to act during an emergency.
